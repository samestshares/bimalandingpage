our product

BIMA is a revolutionary solution to the traditional insurance mechanism with all the benefit of Blockchain throught the power and leverage of Binance Smart Chain and Smart Contract to facilitate all transaction and claims.

BIMA gives users the the leverage to create insurance policy on the Blockchain Ecosystem

BIMA enables users to be able to pay monthly subscription to the Smart Contracts for their insurance policy. The premiums are paid using bima native token bim

Take control with the optional feature that deducts premiums automaticaly from user wallet.
